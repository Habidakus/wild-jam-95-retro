Isolate Your Vector Graphics
  Because vector graphics turn into standard textures upon import, Godot will treat them like pixel art unless you tell it otherwise. To keep your vector graphics smooth:
  - Select your vector files (like SVG) in the FileSystem dock.
  - Go to the Import tab (next to the Scene tab in the top-left).
  - Change the Texture Filter option specifically for these files from Nearest to Linear.
  - Click Reimport.

Scale Your Vectors Before Filtering
  Because Godot rasterizes vectors on load, a small SVG scaled up inside a 2D scene will look blurry.
  - In the Import tab for your SVG files, find the Scale option.
  - Increase this scale (e.g., 2.0 or 4.0) so the vector imports at a massive, high-quality base size.
  - Scale it down inside your 2D scene. It will now look beautifully smooth next to your crisp pixel art.
